Data Source

Miljødirektoratet's Norske Utslipp register (norskeutslipp.no), scraped April 15 2026. Reporting year per facility varies — most recent available (typically 2022–2023 for air emissions). CompanyIDs where known link directly to facility pages.

Scoring Methodology

Suitability 1–10 composite: CO₂ volume 40% (log-scaled, 100 kt → ~4, 1 Mt → ~7, 2 Mt → ~8); biogenic share 20%; port/logistics access 20%; industrial cluster maturity 20%. CO₂-eq = CO₂ + N₂O×298 + CH₄×28 (GWP-100).

Biogenic Method

"Reported" = fossil/biogenic split directly from norskeutslipp.no. "Estimated" = category defaults applied: waste-to-energy 50%, district heating 60%, pulp/paper 80%, biorefinery 90%, biogas/biomass 100%; fossil industries 0%. ETS cross-check: facilities where ETS verified emissions differ from reported CO₂ by >15% are flagged.
